Welcome to
Piece and Freedom Bakery!

At Piece and Freedom Bakery, we believe in the power of delightful treats to bring joy and unite communities. Our artisanal bakery is more than just a place to savor mouthwatering pastries; it's a celebration of life's little moments, a haven of flavors where every bite liberates your senses.

Crafted with Love, Baked with Passion

We are more than just a bakery; we are a community built on sharing moments of laughter, love, and freedom. So come, take a seat, and experience the magic we have to offer.

Europien Cookies

A delightful assortment of traditional European cookies, each with its own unique flavor and texture.

Flourless Chocolate Cake

No flour? No problem. This decadent chocolate cake is a win, something in between a chocolate mousse and a chocolate lava cake, leaving your guests hungry for another silky slice.

Honey Cake

Multiple layers of thin layers of honey sponge soaked with chantilly cream for perfect softness.

Napoleon Torte

Multiple layers of pastry layers intermixed with vanilla custard creme, commonly called Plombir creme in Ukraine.

Tiramisu

An Italian heavenly combination of savoiardi cookies, mascarpone cream, coffee, and an additional swing of Baileys elevates the experience.

Pavlova

Originating in either Australia or New Zealand in the early 20th century, it was named after the Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova. Taking the form of a cake-like circular block of baked meringue, pavlova has a crisp crust and soft, light inside filled with fresh berries like strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and cherries.
