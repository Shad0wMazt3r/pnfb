Europien Cookies

A delightful assortment of traditional European cookies, each with its own unique flavor and texture. From buttery shortbread to delicate almond macarons, these cookies are sure to delight your taste buds and transport you to the charming streets of Europe.

Chocolate Cake

Indulge in the richness of our classic chocolate cake. Layers of moist chocolate sponge and decadent chocolate ganache make this cake an absolute treat for chocolate lovers.

Honey Cake

Multiple layers of thin honey sponge soaked with chantilly cream for perfect softness. This cake is a sweet and airy delight that will leave you craving more.

Napoleon Torte

Multiple layers of pastry intermixed with vanilla custard creme. This European classic is known for its delicate layers and creamy filling.

Tiramisu Cake

An Italian heavenly combination of savoiardi cookies, mascarpone cream, coffee, and a swing of Baileys to elevate the experience.

Pavlova

Originating in either Australia or New Zealand, it was named after the Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova. This cake-like circular block of baked meringue has a crisp crust and soft, light inside filled with fresh berries like strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and cherries.

Creme Caramel

A velvety smooth custard topped with rich caramel sauce. This classic European dessert is the perfect balance of sweetness and creaminess.

Creme Brulie

A decadent dessert featuring a creamy custard base topped with a layer of caramelized sugar. The satisfying crack of the sugar crust is what makes this dessert truly delightful.

Kyiv Cake

A staple of the Ukrainian capital. Light merengue layers with cashews and hazelnuts held together by condensed milk buttercream. Highly decorated with gold elements and condensed buttercream flowers.

Opera Cake

A French classic made with layers of almond sponge, potent coffee syrup, French buttercream, and chocolate ganache. It’s worthy of any special occasion.

Merengue Roulade with Berries

Berries and mascarpone cream are rolled and encased in this easy meringue roulade – crispy on the outside, and soft on the inside.

Croissant

A classic European pastry with flaky layers and a buttery taste. Perfect for breakfast or as a snack with a cup of coffee.

The Catalyzer

A modern twist on a classic European treat. This fusion dessert combines traditional flavors with a contemporary flair, resulting in a unique and delightful experience.

Shooting Stars

These delightful star-shaped cookies are not only visually appealing but also incredibly delicious. Each bite is filled with the sweet harmony of flavors from Europe.

Neptune

Dive into the depths of flavor with our Neptune dessert. This ocean-inspired treat brings together the finest European ingredients to create a wave of taste in every bite.

The 400 Blows

A whirlwind of flavors awaits you in this European dessert. Named after the classic French film, it combines the essence of multiple European cuisines into a delightful sweet experience.
